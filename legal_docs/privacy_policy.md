# GAUVA MOBILITY SERVICES – Comprehensive Legal and Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** December 2025

This Comprehensive Legal and Privacy Policy (“Policy”) describes the terms, conditions, and procedures relating to the collection, use, disclosure, and protection of your information when you use the **Gauva Partner (Driver)** mobile application, website, and related services (“Platform”) operated by **GAUVA MOBILITY SERVICES** (“we”, “our”, or “us”).

This Policy has been drafted in accordance with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, the Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Data or Information) Rules, 2011, and other applicable Indian laws. It also draws guidance from global data protection principles, including the EU’s General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

By accessing or using the Platform, you agree to be bound by this Policy, including all policies incorporated by reference herein.

## 1. Your Consent
By using the **Gauva Partner** Platform, you consent to the collection, processing, use, storage, and disclosure of your personal information as described in this Policy. You also consent to receive transactional and service-related communications from us. If you do not agree, please discontinue the use of the Platform immediately.

## 2. Information We Collect
We collect the following categories of information to facilitate and improve our services:
- **Personal Information:** Name, mobile number, email address, and residential address.
- **Identity & KYC Documents:** Driver's License, Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, and Profile Picture to verify identity.
- **Vehicle Information:** Registration Certificate (RC), Insurance details, vehicle model, and photos.
- **Location Information (Critical):** Precise real-time GPS-based location (Foreground and Background) to support ride matching, navigation, and safety tracking.
- **Financial Information:** Bank account details and UPI IDs for processing driver payouts/earnings (no card details stored).
- **Device and Log Information:** IP address, device model, OS version, and crash data essential for app performance and security.

## 3. Use of Information
We use the collected information for the following lawful purposes:
- To authenticate user identity and verify eligibility to drive (KYC).
- To facilitate ride bookings and match you with nearby riders.
- To process payouts and manage your earnings wallet.
- To send service updates, push notifications, new ride alerts, and SMS.
- To maintain internal records, improve platform performance, and route optimization.
- To comply with applicable legal obligations or respond to lawful requests.
- To ensure safety, fraud prevention, and system security.

## 4. Disclosure and Sharing of Information
We may disclose your information under the following limited circumstances:
- **To Riders:** Name, photo, vehicle details, and live location shared solely for ride coordination and safety.
- **To Trusted Service Providers:** Third parties such as **Google Maps** (navigation), **Firebase** (notifications/analytics), and Payment Gateways (secure payouts).
- **To Law Enforcement:** Or governmental authorities where required by applicable law.
- **To Protect Rights:** To protect our legal rights, users, or the integrity of the Platform.
*We do not sell, trade, or rent user information to any third parties.*

## 5. Data Retention and Deletion
- **User Data:** Retained only as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected or as required by law (e.g., transaction records).
- **Account Deletion:** Users can request deletion via the App settings or by emailing **support@gauvaservices.in**.
- **Process:** Upon request, personal identification data is erased from active databases within 15 business days, subject to retention required for legal/fraud-prevention purposes.

## 6. Data Security Measures
We employ organizational, technical, and administrative safeguards to protect personal information, including:
- **Encryption:** AES-level encryption for sensitive data transfer.
- **Access Control:** Restricted to authorized personnel only.
- **Secure Infrastructure:** Data storage on secure cloud servers complying with safety standards.
- **Regular Audits:** Adherence to reasonable security practices.
*While we maintain strict protocols, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and users acknowledge this inherent risk.*

## 7. Your Rights
Users have the right to:
- Access, review, and correct their personal data.
- Withdraw consent to data processing (permissions), though this may limit App functionality.
- Request deletion of their personal data.
- Lodge complaints with our support team regarding data handling.
